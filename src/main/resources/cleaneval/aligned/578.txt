TRANSFUSION FROM-BABY BROTHER PROVES SUCCESSFUL---------------------------------------------------------------K.C. HOWARD---------------------------- - STAFF REPORTER--------------------------- WITH HER BROTHER'S BONE MARROW CELLS THRIVING INSIDE HER, SIX-YEAR-OLD MOLLY-NASH RETURNED HOME TO ENGLEWOOD, COLO. AFTER 118 DAYS AT FAIRVIEW-UNIVERSITY MEDICAL CENTER.------------------------ MOLLY RECEIVED A LIFE-SAVING BLOOD TRANSFUSION-FROM HER NEWLY BORN BROTHER ADAM-ON SEPT. 2-, WHICH SPARED HER FROM A FIGHT AGAINST FANCONI ANEMIA.------------------------ THE RARE RECESSIVE DISEASE CAUSES BONE MARROW FAILURE IN ITS VICTIMS, DETERIORATING THEIR IMMUNE SYSTEMS.----------------------- THIS POTENTIALLY FATAL ILLNESS CAUSED SEVERAL BIRTH DEFECTS IN MOLLY, INCLUDING TWO HOLES IN HER HEART-AND NO THUMBS OR HIP SOCKETS. SHE WAS ALSO BORN WITHOUT A PORTION OF HER BRAIN.----------------------- "MOLLY KNOWS MORE ABOUT LIFE THAN ANY CHILD HER AGE SHOULD," SAID- SARAH YOUNGERMAN, THE PUBLIC RELATIONS COORDINATOR FOR THE ACADEMIC HEALTH CENTER.----------------------- AFTER EIGHT RECONSTRUCTIVE SURGERIES, MOLLY'S BIRTH DEFECTS WERE NO LONGER INHIBITING. HER WEAK IMMUNE SYSTEM, HOWEVER, WAS THREATENING HER SURVIVAL.------------------------ TO BEAT THIS POTENTIALLY FATAL DISEASE, UNIVERSITY DOCTORS USED UMBILICAL CORD BLOOD FROM ADAM. HIS HEALTHY BLOOD CELLS THEN DESTROYED AND TOOK OVER MOLLY'S MALIGNANT BONE MARROW, ALLOWING HER TO EFFICIENTLY PRODUCE RED BLOOD-CELLS, WHITE BLOOD CELLS AND PLATELETS.----------------------- THIS TRANSFUSION WAS THE FIRST OF ITS KIND BECAUSE MOLLY'S CURE -- HER BROTHER ADAM -- WAS GENETICALLY SELECTED AS A HEALTHY EMBRYO AND PERFECT BLOOD DONOR FOR MOLLY.----------------------- GENETIC SELECTION OF CHILDREN IS CONTROVERSIAL, BUT YOUNGERMAN SAID THAT MOST PEOPLE ARE VERY-SUPPORTIVE-OF-THE NASHES' DECISION AND ARE HAPPY TO HEAR THAT MOLLY IS RECOVERING MORE QUICKLY THAN EXPECTED.----------------------- "ADAM'S BONE-MARROW HAS COMPLETELY TAKEN OVER AND HER WHITE CELLS ARE CURED," SAID UNIVERSITY CORD BLOOD SPECIALIST DR. JOHN WAGNER, WHO PERFORMED THE PROCEDURE.------------------------ HE ADDED THAT ALTHOUGH THE TRANSFUSION-INCREASED MOLLY'S CHANCES OF SURVIVAL, SHE WILL STILL BE MORE RECEPTIVE TO CANCER THAN MOST PEOPLE.----------------------- MOLLY WILL RETURN TO MINNESOTA FOR CHECKUPS IN APRIL AND SEPTEMBER.------------------------ TO PROTECT HERSELF FROM INFECTIONS-THAT COULD-DAMAGE HER HEALING-IMMUNE SYSTEM, MOLLY MUST STAY INDOORS AND-WEAR A MASK UNTIL HER IMMUNE SYSTEM-HAS COMPLETELY-RECOVERED.-------------------------MOLLY IS A VERY-SOPHISTICATED GIRL," YOUNGERMAN SAID, "THOUGH SHE KNOWS SHE CAN'T BE EXPOSED TO PEOPLE, EVEN IF SHE MISSES OUT ON GOING TO RESTAURANTS WITH HER-FAMILY.-----------------------" BUT-IN THE FALL,-YOUNGERMAN-SAID, MOLLY LOOKS FORWARD TO ATTENDING SECOND GRADE AT A-LOCAL SCHOOL.----------------------------------------------- K.C.-HOWARD WELCOMES COMMENTS
