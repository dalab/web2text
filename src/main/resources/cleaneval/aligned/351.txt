AN END TO THE UNFAIR POLITICAL INFLUENCE WHICH ARMS COMPANIES HAVE ON GOVERNMENT POLICY.------- THE REVOLVING DOOR BETWEEN GOVERNMENT AND ARMS COMPANIES IS ONE OF THE MECHANISMS THAT ALLOWS ARMS
