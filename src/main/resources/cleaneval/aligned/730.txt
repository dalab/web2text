DURING SUMMER AND FALL, ADULT AND JUVENILE FISH-------------------- UNDERTAKE WIDE RANGING FEEDING MIGRATIONS ALONG THE COAST, OFTEN-------------------- TRAVELING SEVERAL HUNDRED KILOMETERS BEYOND THEIR NATAL RIVERS.-------------------- IN LATE FALL STRIPED BASS ASCEND THE RIVERS TO OVER-WINTER IN FRESH-------------------- WATER, IN ORDER TO AVOID LOW LETHAL MARINE TEMPERATURES. IN OTHER WORDS, STRIPED BASS-------------------- DO NOT NECESSARILY OVER-WINTER IN THEIR-SPAWNING RIVERS.
